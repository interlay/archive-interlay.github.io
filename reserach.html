Research

Technological advantage stems from persistent and rigorous research. At Interlay, we are hence dedicated to contribute to cutting-edge research in the areas of blockchain interoperability, security, and crypto-economics.

As of today, our team members have co-authored 30 scientific publications counting over 400 citations, while at / in collaboration with leading universities and research centres around the globe.

We are advocates of open access research and believe in sharing our findings with the community.
